⚔ Attack Cheatsheet — Cold Iron Ranseur +1 (2d4 ×3, two-handed, reach, disarm)

Click any attack to roll it. +12 to hit = BAB 7 + STR 3 + enh 1 + Weapon Focus 1. Power Attack: +6 dmg (two-handed), –2 to hit (negated on 1st attack by Furious Focus).

🧭 What To Use When

  • Standing still, enemy in reach → Full Attack + Power Attack. +12 then +5, both 2d4+13. Your bread & butter.
  • Had to move this turn → Standard + Power Attack. +12 for 2d4+13 (Furious Focus = no penalty).
  • Enemy approaching → Let them eat an AoO at 10 ft: +12 for 2d4+7. You get 5 per round.
  • Two enemies adjacent to each other → Cleave — hit one, free swing on its neighbor (–2 AC till next turn).
  • Need to shut down a big threat → Dazing Assault — –5 to hit, but a hit dazes for 1 round (Fort DC 17).
  • Enemy with a nasty weapon → Disarm with the ranseur: CMB +12 (+2 from ranseur) vs their CMD.

📌 Don't Forget

  • You have 10 ft reach — you threaten enemies 10 ft away, not adjacent. Hitting an adjacent foe takes a –4 (improvised) penalty.
  • 5 attacks of opportunity per round (Combat Reflexes + DEX 4). Punish anyone moving past you — even while flat-footed.
  • Cold iron weapon — bypasses DR on fey and demons.
  • Furious Focus only negates the Power Attack penalty on your first attack each turn.
  • Bodyguard: spend an AoO to give an adjacent ally +2 AC (aid another).
  • Default round: Full Attack + Power Attack → +12 / +5, both 2d4+13.
